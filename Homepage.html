Welcome Math Wizards

🧠 Problem-Solving & Challenges - Weekly Math Puzzles: Share logic puzzles, riddles, or brain teasers. Let members compete or collaborate to solve them. - Math Olympiad Practice: Prepare for competitions with past problems and timed drills. - Escape Room Math: Create a math-themed escape room with clues that require solving equations or logic puzzles.

🎲 Games & Interactive Fun - Math Jeopardy: Use categories like Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, and History of Math. - 24 Game Tournament: Players use four numbers and basic operations to make 24. - Math Bingo: Customize bingo cards with math problems instead of numbers.

🎨 Creative & Hands-On Activities - Build Polyhedra: Use paper, straws, or 3D software to construct geometric shapes. - Math Art Projects: Explore fractals, tessellations, or golden ratio-inspired designs. - Origami & Geometry: Teach geometric principles through paper folding.

📚 Learning & Exploration - Guest Speakers: Invite mathematicians, data scientists, or engineers to talk about real-world applications. - Math Movie Night: Watch films like Hidden Figures, The Man Who Knew Infinity, or A Beautiful Mind. - Explore Math History: Present on famous mathematicians or breakthroughs.

🌐 Community & Outreach - Math Mentoring: Pair older students with younger ones for tutoring or math games. - Math Fair: Host an event with booths showcasing fun math concepts. - Social Media Challenges: Post weekly math problems or fun facts to engage a wider audience.

🏆 Competitions & Recognition - Monthly Math Bee: Fast-paced quiz-style competition. - Leaderboard System: Track points for participation, problem-solving, and creativity. - Math Awards Ceremony: Celebrate achievements with certificates or small prizes.
